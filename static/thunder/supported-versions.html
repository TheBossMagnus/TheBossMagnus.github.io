Click on any supported version to go directly to its download page on Modrinth.

What does each status mean?

  • ✅ Recommended: Most stable, performant, and up-to-date release.
  • 🟢 Actively supported: Up-to-date release with the newest mods.
  • ⏳ Long-term support: Releases for older Minecraft versions with fewer updates, less optimizations.
  • 🧪 Experimental: Unstable version, may have bugs.
  • ⚠️ Out of support: No longer supported or updated, but still available to download.
  • 🔴 Not Available: Not available for this loader/version combination.

Versions become deprecated when one or more of the following happens:

  1. They have low downloads and user count, or their maintenance is not worth the effort.
  2. They receive little to no mods updates and support.
  3. They have some critical issue and Mojang has already released an hotfix update.
